How It Started

Our journey from an idea to a movement for change.

🌱 The Spark

It all began with a simple observation - every day, perfectly good food was being thrown away while many families struggled to afford a meal. Our team realized that the problem wasn’t a lack of food, but a lack of connection.

💡 The Idea

We asked ourselves: What if technology could bridge that gap? That question became the seed of our project - a smart, easy to use web platform that connects food donors directly with food banks in real time.

🧭 The Research

We analyzed existing solutions an realized, that while they addressed food waste globally, none offered a simple, local, non-profit system for direct food donations. This insight shaped our unique goal: build a community driven donation network.

🤝 The Collaboration

Our team ethusiastically worked on this project, combining our skills in design, coding and project management. Every step was guided by ethical research and community feedback to ensure transparency, privacy and fairness.

🌍 The Vision Ahead

What began as a university project has grown into a vision for real impact - reducing food waste, supporting sustainability, and empowering local communities.
