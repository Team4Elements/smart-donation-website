Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start donating food?
Visit the "Donate Food" section from your dashboard, browse current needs from food banks, and make a donation promise. The food bank will review and confirm your donation.
How does the points system work?
You earn points for every donation: 1 point per €1 donated, 2 points per food item, and 5 points per volunteer hour. Points help you level up and earn badges!
Can I volunteer remotely?
Yes! Many food banks need help with administrative tasks, social media, and coordination that can be done remotely. Check the volunteer opportunities for remote options.
How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox to reset your password.
