Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start donating money? ⌄
Click the "Start Donating" button on the home page or navigate to the Donations section. Enter the amount you wish to donate, select your payment method, and confirm your contribution. You'll receive a confirmation email and points will be added to your account.
What payment methods do you accept? ⌄
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All transactions are secure and encrypted for your safety.
Can I set up recurring donations? ⌄
Yes! When making a donation, you can choose to make it recurring on a weekly, monthly, or yearly basis. You can manage or cancel recurring donations anytime from your account dashboard.
How do I donate food from my store? ⌄
Visit the "Donate Food" section from your dashboard. Enter your store details and location, then mark your store on the map. Your location will appear on the main map on the home page, making it easy for those in need to find food donations near them.
What types of food can I donate? ⌄
You can donate non-perishable items, fresh produce, packaged goods, and prepared meals. Please ensure all food items meet safety standards and are within their expiration dates. Contact our support team if you have questions about specific items.
How does the map location work? ⌄
When you register your store for food donations, you'll place a pin on the interactive map. This pin shows your exact location, operating hours, and available food types. Users can click on your pin to see more details and get directions.
How can I volunteer my time? ⌄
Navigate to the "Volunteer" section in your account. You can browse available opportunities, select the ones that interest you, and specify how many hours you'd like to commit. Each hour volunteered earns you points and contributes to the leaderboard.
What volunteer opportunities are available? ⌄
Opportunities include food sorting and distribution, administrative tasks, social media support, event coordination, delivery assistance, and community outreach. Check the volunteer section regularly as new opportunities are added weekly.
Can I volunteer remotely? ⌄
Yes! Many tasks can be done remotely, including administrative support, social media management, and coordination work. Filter volunteer opportunities by "Remote" to see available options.
How does the points system work? ⌄
You earn points for every donation (1 point per $1 donated), food donations (points based on value), and volunteer hours (5 points per hour). Points are displayed on your dashboard and contribute to your position on the leaderboard.
What can I do with my points? ⌄
Points showcase your contribution to the community and improve your ranking on the leaderboard. High-performing contributors receive recognition badges, certificates, and may be featured in our monthly newsletter.
How do I access my account dashboard? ⌄
Click on the "Dashboard" button in the top navigation bar. Your dashboard displays your total donations (money and food), volunteer hours, points earned, badges, and your current leaderboard position.
Can I track my donation history? ⌄
Yes! Your dashboard includes a complete history of all your contributions, including money donations, food donations, and volunteer hours. You can filter by date range and export your data for tax purposes.
How does the leaderboard work? ⌄
The leaderboard ranks individuals and organizations based on their total points earned. It's updated in real-time and can be filtered to show daily, weekly, monthly, or all-time top contributors. Both individuals and organizations have separate leaderboards.
How can I improve my leaderboard ranking? ⌄
Increase your ranking by making regular donations, volunteering more hours, and encouraging others to join 4elements. Organizations can coordinate team efforts to climb the organizational leaderboard.
How do I reset my password? ⌄
Click "Login" in the top right, then select "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox to reset your password securely.
Can organizations create accounts? ⌄
Absolutely! Organizations can register for a dedicated account with additional features like team management, bulk food donation coordination, and enhanced analytics. Contact our team for organizational account setup.
